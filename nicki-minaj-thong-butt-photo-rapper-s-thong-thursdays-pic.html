Publish date: 2024-08-02

Nicki Minaj is starting a new social media trend: Thong Thursdays. The 30-year-old rapper posted a photo of her behind on Instagram Thursday, July 18.

"#ThongThursdays," she captioned the close-up shot of her neon pink thong. In the photo, Minaj stands with her back to the camera and appears to be nude except for the one undergarment.

Earlier in the week, the "Super Bass" singer also tried to get another trend started. "#CamelToe Wednesdays," she captioned another Instagram photo on July 17. But Minaj must have changed her mind about sharing the intimate photo, as she later deleted the image.

Minaj has recently been sharing a lot of sexy photos of herself with her Instagram followers. On July 17, she posted a snapshot of herself flaunting her curves while squatting on the ledge of a pool in high heels, a floral swimsuit and sun hat.
