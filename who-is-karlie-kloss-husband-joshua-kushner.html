Who Is Karlie Kloss Husband, Joshua Kushner?

Publish date: 2024-07-10

When it comes to the world of finance, there are only a few select names that just about anyone could recognize, Kushner, being one of them. The entire family has done incredibly well for themselves, however, it was the youngest, Joshua Kushner, that had all eyes on him when he and model, Karlie Kloss tied the knot back in 2018. The Kushner family has extensive ties to the Trump family, especially after the eldest brother, Jared Kushner married Ivanka Trump.

Although the family differs when it comes to aspects of politics, their finance game remains as strong as ever. Joshua has managed to find himself worth a whopping $800 million, leaving many fans of Karlie to wonder exactly who the finance mogul is, and how he made his millions. Let's find out!

Who Is Joshua Kushner?

Joshua Kushner first came to prominence as a young adult. He was born to parents Charles and Seryl Kushner, who are major names in the world of finance. The Kushner family is well-known across the United States and has a tight inner circle, mainly made up of those living in Manhattan. While we haven't heard much of the youngest son, Joshua, that all changed after he married the world-renowned fashion model, Karlie Kloss. The two were first romantically linked back in 2012, and dated for well over 5 years before saying their "I do's".

This caused quite the controversy amongst fans of Kloss' as the Kushner's are close friends and business partners with the Trumps, so much that Joshua's older brother, Jared, married Donald Trump's daughter, Ivanka Trump, back in 2009. This led many to believe that Karlie and Joshua held the same political beliefs as their in-laws, however, that doesn't appear to be the case.

Karlie has made it very clear that she and her hubby, Joshua, do not see eye-to-eye with her in-laws when it comes to politics. Karlie made this revelation on Andy Cohen's 'Watch What Happens Live', and further showcased her admiration for the Democratic party after posting an 'I Voted' pic on her Instagram earlier in November.

As for Joshua himself, the financier is worth a whopping $800 million! He made his fortune thanks to his family's real estate portfolio, which is set to be worth well over $7 billion. As for his own personal accomplishments, Joshua appears to have done it all. After graduating from Harvard University, Kushner went on to work at Goldman Sachs, where he used his experience there to then create Thrive Capital in 2009. This didn't mark the end of Joshua's business ventures.

He later went on to become the co-founder of Oscar Insurance and Cadre, which works directly with high net worth investors with a lot of real estate to work with. It is no secret that Joshua and his family name have done wonders for him, however, his work ethic is what ultimately made him as successful as he is today.
