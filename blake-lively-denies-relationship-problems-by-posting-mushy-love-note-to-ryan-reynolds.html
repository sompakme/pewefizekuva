Blake Lively Denies Relationship Problems By Posting Mushy Love Note To Ryan Reynolds

Highlights

  • Blake Lively praised Ryan Reynolds for his new movie, expressing admiration and love for her husband.
  • Rumors about their marriage's longevity due to past relationships and questionable beginnings have surfaced.
  • Despite controversies, Blake and Ryan have maintained a strong relationship since marrying in 2012.

Like many celebrity couples, Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds are routinely the subject of rumors speculating about the status of their relationship. While they’ve faced their fair share of break-up allegations in the past, Blake seemed to shut down any suspicions with a lovey-dovey post about her husband to celebrate his new movie.

Blake Had A Sweet Show Of Support For Her Husband’s New Movie

On Wednesday, April 10, Blake Lively took to her Instagram Story to share a heartfelt message about her husband while promoting his upcoming movie, If. The 36-year-old actress expressed admiration for her 47-year-old spouse, describing him as everything she had ever dreamed of. Alongside her touching words, Blake posted the trailer for the new live action/animated comedy crossover film.

“Everything I believed as a kid did turn out to be true… his name is @vancityreynolds,” she wrote in the caption. “Well, his legal name isn’t, At Vancity Reynolds.” Blake added, “But he is dreamy and he is in @ifmovie, which is also pure magic.”

If releases on May 17th and will star Reynolds alongside Cailey Fleming, Emily Blunt, George Clooney, Bradley Cooper, Matt Damon, and Maya Rudolph. After realizing her ability to see everyone's imaginary friends, the movie’s plot follows a young girl who sets off on a whimsical journey to reunite forgotten IFs with their owners.

Why Some Fans Think Blake And Ryan’s Marriage Is Doomed

While Blake is clearly excited for her husband’s upcoming movie, the Gossip Girl alum didn’t acknowledge the various rumors circulating about her marriage, including a report by Nicki Swift that claims the couple likely won’t last in the long-term.

Firstly, rumors surrounding Reynolds' previous marriage to Scarlett Johansson and the timing of his relationship with Lively raise questions about fidelity, suggesting a potential shadow from the past that may cast doubt on the solidity of their union. While no one has ever claimed cheating, Reynolds met Blake while working on Green Lantern, while he was still married to Scarlett. He allegedly started dating his current wife before his divorce was finalized.

Additionally, the questions about fidelity have further been fueled after Reynolds confessed he and Blake were both on a double date with other people before realizing their mutual attraction, indicating a somewhat unconventional start to their relationship.
