Jessica Biel stunned at this year's Met Gala as she walked the red carpet all on her own. She wore a beautiful pink dress covered in pink petals, following the theme of the Garden of Time to the letter. Her wavy hair perfectly complemented her whimsy look, and she turned heads as soon as she walked in.

Sadly, Justin Timberlake couldn't walk the red carpet with her, but he didn't miss the chance to admire his beautiful wife.

After the Gala, Jessica Biel posted a reel on her Instagram, showing the before and after as she got ready for the big event. She accompanied the post with Michael Bublé's "Feeling Good," and captioned it with "And I’m feeling... GREAT." The actress was also wearing a Cartier gold cuff necklace with a matching ring and showed off her silver clutch.

"Thank you @cartier for the stunning jewels," she commented on her own post.

Several people left their gushing comments, complimenting her beauty and her stylish outfit. Justin Timberlake was no exception. While he couldn't appreciate the look in person at the Gala, he was stunned by the video and left three heart-eyes emojis in the comments.

Justin Timberlake's Absence Stirred Rumors Of Marriage Problems

While seeing Jessica Biel walk the red carpet by herself raised some suspicions in fans, Justin Timberlake actually had a good reason to ditch the event. The singer was in San Jose, as he had a show scheduled for May 6.

Still, worries about marriage troubles remained, especially considering how, before he went on tour, Jessica was reportedly worried his touring would hurt their marriage. "Jessica is a bit worried about the tour," a source told Us Weekly. "If Justin were to have another incident, it would be the last straw for Jessica." The incident they're referring to, of course, is the time Justin Timberlake was caught in a compromising position with his Palmer co-star Alisha Wainwright.
