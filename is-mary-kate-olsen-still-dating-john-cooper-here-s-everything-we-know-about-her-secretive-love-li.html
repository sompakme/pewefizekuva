Are Mary-Kate Olsen And John Cooper Still In A Relationship?

The Olsen twins hoped to leave fame behind and have created incredibly impressive lives for themselves. They seem to genuinely enjoy designing clothing and have several successful lines to prove it, from Elizabeth and James to The Row.

Of course, many people have been fans of the Olsens for a long time. And even though they don't want to share much about their lives with the public anymore, fans hope that Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen are both happy in their love lives. Ashley got married to Louis Eisner in 2022... but is Mary-Kate still in a relationship? After watching the twins act in so many movies for so many years, it's hard not be interested in their well-being.

In February 2021, Mary-Kate Olsen was linked to John Cooper. According to E! News, Cooper is the Brightwire CEO, a company that analyzes financial news. They were spotted at the restaurant Estiatorio Milos in New York City on February 26th, 2021.
