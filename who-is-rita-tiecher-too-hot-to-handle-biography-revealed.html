Who Is Rita Tiecher Too Hot To Handle? Biography Revealed

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Rita Tiecher is a model and a Disk Jockey (DJ) from Brazil, currently casting on the reality TV series “Too Hot To Handle Brazil”.

The show “Too Hot To Handle” follows the premise of 10 adults living together in a house for 4 weeks with a virtual assistant named “Lana”, where they go through various workshops and are banned from any sexual contact, kissing, or self-gratification. The penalty is the reduction of the grand prize of $100,000 for every misconduct, the grand prize is split between the remaining participants at the end.
