Claudia Jordan Confirms Jamie Foxx, Katie Holmes Are Dating

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Spill the tea, hunni! Former Real Housewives of Atlanta star Claudia Jordan confirmed that her pal and one-time rumored love interest Jamie Foxx is dating Katie Holmes.

The radio personality reality star, 43, appeared on the Monday, June 20, edition of the podcast, “Allegedly with Theo Von & Matthew Cole Weiss,” where she denied that she and Foxx, 48, were once an item, but admitted that her “good friend” is indeed courting Holmes, 37.

“He is very happy with her,” Jordan raved of Foxx’s notoriously private relationship with the Dawson’s Creek alum. “I like that he seems very happy.”

Us Weekly exclusively revealed back in 2015 that the Django Unchained actor and Holmes had been secretly dating since October 2013.

Last September, the Touched With Fire actress exhibited a concerted effort to keep their romance under wraps by dressing in disguise to meet up with her man. A source told Us that Holmes, who shares daughter Suri, 10, with ex-husband Tom Cruise, “put on a wig and hat to meet him at a hotel” in Los Angeles.

“She said she loved him,” a second insider told Us after the pair were spotted holding hands in March 2015. “She always gushes about how amazing he is and the joy he brings into the world.”

In December 2015, TMZ reported that Foxx celebrated his 48th birthday with Holmes by his side. The “Blame It” singer was joined by his lady love and a slew of other famous faces including Alicia Keys and Jennifer Hudson.
