But who is Hilaria Baldwin? Even before meeting Alec, she had been doing very well for herself, and through the years, she has single-handedly built an impressive net worth. Let's see how she's done it.

6 Who Is Hilaria Baldwin, And How Much Is She Worth?

Hilaria Thomas Baldwin was born in Boston, Massachusetts, the daughter of Kathryn Hayward and David Thomas. Kathryn was an associate physician at Massachusetts General Hospital and assistant professor of medicine at Harvard Medical School, while her dad was an attorney. Hilaria's parents founded an integrative health organization, and considering the path she took in her career, it makes sense that she would come from a family that is mindful of maintaining a healthy lifestyle and helping others do the same. Hilaria is a yoga instructor and a wellness specialist, as well as an entrepreneur, and has amassed a $10 million net worth.

5 When Did Hilaria Meet Alec Baldwin?

Hilaria met Alec Baldwin in 2011, and it was a completely random meeting. They were both at a restaurant in New York, and it was practically love at first sight. "I wandered into Pure Food and Wine on Irving Place, on an unseasonably warm February evening," Alec shared with the world. "My friend, @bocartist (Brendan O’Connell) and I sat down (outside!) and as I turned to my left I saw a woman who would change my life. Entirely."

Apparently, he knew immediately that he wanted to be with her, and while it took Hilaria a bit by surprise, she liked him and decided to give him a chance. "I was standing near the door with my friends when he walked up and took my hand and said, 'I must know you'," Hilaria said. Alec gave her his card, and a couple of days later, she called him to go on a date.

4 What Encouraged Hilaria Baldwin To Pursue Her Career

Hilaria built her incredible net worth by being, among other things, a very successful health practitioner. And while it's a genuine, positive passion of hers, it came from a very difficult period of her life. She struggled with an aggressive eating disorder, and after years of experiencing bad health, she found a way out through the work she still does to this day.
