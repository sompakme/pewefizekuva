About Howard Stern’s Wife Beth Ostrosky

Beth Ostrosky was an actress and received her first role in the 1996 film Flirting with Disaster as one of the daughters of Ben Stiller's supposed birth parents. She later went on to have a more prominent role in the film Whipped. Beth made for television debut in the final season of the G4 show Filter, and shortly after that went on to the Spike TV series Casino Cinema.

Beth has also been in a handful of modeling jobs including her own line of calendars. She has also been on the cover of the For Him Magazine three times. Beth has been voted for by readers of the For Him Magazine as one of 100 Sexiest Women of the Year. She won this honor in 2002, 2003, 2004, and 2007. She has been 96th on the list for Most Desirable Woman in 2007 for AskMen.com
