Cameron Nicholas Douglas McIntosh (born 2 December 1975 in Pietermaritzburg) is a South African slalom canoer who competed from the late 1990s to the late 2000s. He was eliminated in the qualifying round of the C-2 event at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, finishing in 12th place.
