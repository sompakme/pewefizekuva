Hallmark Channel Stars Reveal the Sequels They Are Down to Make

Publish date: 2024-09-06

The Hallmark Channel keeps viewers hooked all year long with its feel-good films and annual “Countdown to Christmas” programming — but which movies do the network’s stars want made into sequels?

Andrew Walker, Nikki DeLoach, Aimee Teegarden and more Hallmark personalities exclusively told Us Weekly which films they want to revisit. Both DeLoach and Teegarden, for example, picked a movie in which they acted alongside Walker.

“They’re all so wonderful and special in their own right. The one that I did last year is coming to mind — the one that I did with Andrew Walker. It was really fun,” Teegarden exclusively told Us in October 2022 about 2021’s My Christmas Family Tree. “I would love to see what they would do with a sequel.”

DeLoach, meanwhile, has a pitch ready to revisit 2016’s A Dream of Christmas, which followed her character, Penny, making a wish to be single again amid a lull in her marriage to Walker’s Stuart.
