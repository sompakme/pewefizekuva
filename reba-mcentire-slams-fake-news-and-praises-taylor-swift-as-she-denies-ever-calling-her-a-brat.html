A Facebook Post Accused Reba Of Insulting Taylor

It's no wonder the country singer wanted to set the record straight about what she said about Taylor Swift. The reports of what Reba McEntire had allegedly said were very harsh, and not something anyone would want people to think you said.

A Facebook page made a viral post with fake statements from the singer, saying that "Reba McIntire [sic] talks about how disappointed she was to look up at the big screen while performing The National Anthem, only to see Taylor Swift laughing and drinking and making a mockery of the event." The post also said that Reba had stated "I let her have it afterward. She's an entitled little brat."

Considering that the Facebook page didn't even write Reba's name correctly, the chances of the statement being true are very slim, but of course, if something becomes viral, whether or not it's true stops mattering. So, it was a good thing that Reba McEntire clarified it before the rumors got out of hand.
