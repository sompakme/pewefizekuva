Bachelors Ty Brown, Elizabeth Kitt Brown Welcome First Child

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Baby love! Bachelor Nation couple Ty Brown and Elizabeth Kitt Brown welcomed their first child, daughter Blakely Elizabeth Brown, Us Weekly can exclusively reveal.

“Elizabeth and I are so excited to be parents and start another caption in our lives. It has been a long journey to get here, but so worth it,” Ty told Us. “We could not be more thrilled with the 7.5 lbs, 20 inch bundle of joy who has already got dad wrapped around her finger.”

He added, “Elizabeth was a super mom and did a fantastic job during labor, as well as th past nine months in making sure that Blakely was as health as she could be. We are so very blessed.”

Elizabeth, who competed on Jake Pavelka’s Bachelor season 14 in 2010, shared a snapshot of her baby bump on Saturday, April 20, via Instagram. “Any day now,” she wrote alongside a picture of herself lounging in a hammock.

She also celebrated her baby shower last month and took to Instagram to post pictures of the happy occasion.

Elizabeth and Ty, the latter of whom vied for Ali Fedotowsky’s affections on season 6 of The Bachelorette in 2010, revealed a gorgeous image of Blakely’s nursery back in January.

The couple met at a charity event in L.A. and started dating in January 2013. They got engaged nine months later and tied the knot in March 2014.

“The weather was sunny and warm and perfect,” the pair previously told Us of the ceremony. “It really was the perfect day.”
