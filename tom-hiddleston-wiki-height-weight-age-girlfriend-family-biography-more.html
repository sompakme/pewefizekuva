Tom Hiddleston a popular English actor came into prominence after his portrayal of supervillain ‘Loki’ in the 2011 film ‘Thor’. His role as ‘Loki’ was reprised in films such as ‘The Avengers’, ‘Thor: The Dark World’, ‘Thor: Ragnarok’, etc. He was born on February 9, 1981, in Westminster London.

Apart from being an actor, he is also a well-known producer, musical performer, and philanthropist. He visited Sudan to educate people about hunger and malnutrition. In December 2017, his name was mentioned in Debrett’s list of the ‘Most Influential People in the UK’.

In 2006, he made his film debut in the British drama ‘Unrelated’. In the following year, he was featured in ‘The Challenging’, ‘Cymbeline’, and ‘Othello’. He was appreciated by the critics for his role in ‘Cymbeline’ and has won the ‘Laurence Oliver Award’ for ‘Best Newcomer in a Play’.

He appeared in ‘Thor’ which was based on ‘Marvel Comics’ in 2011. The movie turned out to be a commercial success and Tom earned popularity on the international level. He played the lead roles in films like “I saw the light” and “Kong: Skull Island”.

He was also featured in TV series like “A Waste of Shame”, “Victoria Cross Heroes”, “Darwin’s Secret Notebooks”, and “Trollhunters”. He helps in providing work for newcomers and games as he is the ambassador of the “Illuminating BAFTA Campaign”.
