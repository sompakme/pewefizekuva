Katherine Beth Nolan (born January 28, 1987) is an American sports television host. She hosts Always Late with Katie Nolan on ESPN2 as well as a weekly ESPN podcast called Sports? With Katie Nolan. She formerly hosted Garbage Time with Katie Nolan on Fox Sports. She won a Sports Emmy Award in 2016 for Garbage Time and was nominated for another in 2019 for Always Late.
