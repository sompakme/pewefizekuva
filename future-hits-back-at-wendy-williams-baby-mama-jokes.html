Future Hits Back At Wendy Williams Baby Mama Jokes

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Wendy Williams sure knows how to get a reaction out of people. Future is the latest celebrity to publicly respond to Williams for making fun of the alleged drama in his personal life.

As explained during a hot topics segment on The Wendy Williams Show Thursday (Nov. 8), Future is reportedly preparing to welcome a child with Joie Chavis, his fifth baby’s mother.

The parents-to-be celebrated the impending birth at a baby shower last weekend. Making matters more complicated, Future is allegedly romantically involved with another one of his children’s mothers.

“Once upon a time there was a man named Future and Future has a lot of baby’s mothers,” Williams said as a graphic popped up of all five of the women whom he shares children with.

The talk show host carefully went through the list which included Ciara, who “fled the scene” according to Williams. “And now Future junior has a new father,” she said of Ciara and Future’s young son.

Williams also noted that Chavis shares a child with Bow Wow, whose ex-girlfriend is Ciara. “Where are your condoms?” Williams remarked.

The “Transformer” rapper replied on Instagram Friday (Nov. 9) with a photo of himself captioned, “Thank God I’m richer than Wendy Williams.” The Shade Room later pointed out that Future isn’t richer than Williams.
