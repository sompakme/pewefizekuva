10 Celebs Oprah Is Friends With (10 She Refuses To Talk To)

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Oprah Winfrey is one of the most recognizable names in the world! The television broadcaster turned talk show host, turned billionaire is the definition of self-made, and if there is someone who deserves to be praised for the work she has done, it's definitely Oprah.

You may have been part of her book club, watched her hit daytime talk show, or are a fan of the numerous films she's played in, but if there is one thing that is clear, Oprah is one heck of a force to be reckoned with.

Oprah has been in the spotlight for decades now starting her career in journalism before getting her big break with 'The Oprah Winfrey Show' in 1986, since then, she has formed several close relationships with other famous names and we will be diving into that in a bit. Here are 10 celebrities who are friends with Oprah, and 10 she has some bad blood with.

40 Friends: Gayle King

39

Gayle King and Oprah Winfrey have been friends for over 40 years! The two first met at young broadcasters working on the same network back in 1976. Since then, the two have been inseparable. They have been through it all, and have supported one another for the entire lengths of their careers, and are the ultimate #BestFriendGoals.

38 Friends: Michelle LaVaughn Robinson

37

Michelle and Oprah have formed quite a special friendship over the last two decades! Oprah, who is close with the former First Family, has been spotted with Michelle in both public and private settings. Michelle has also been a guest on Oprah's show numerous times, and the two seem to get on just great!

36 Friends: Tyler Perry

35

Oprah Winfrey and Tyler Perry have a friendship that most people would kill for, and it all comes down to their mutual respect for one another. The two have supported one another since the day they first met. Oprah even tributed Perry when he became the first-ever African American to own a major movie studio, says Oprah Magazine, one example of many showcasing their budding friendship.

34 Friends: Ellen DeGeneres

33

Oprah Winfrey essentially passed down her reign as queen of daytime talk show to Ellen DeGeneres, and we could not think of someone more worthy of such a title. Ellen and Oprah have been close for years, and believe it or not, Oprah was the first person to know about Ellen's coming out that took place in 1994, which was revealed on the sitcom show 'Ellen'.

32 Friends: Deepak Chopra

31

Oprah and Deepak Chopra are two names that you'd kill to have at your dinner party! Deepak Chopra, a prominent figure in the 'new age movement' has written countless books on the topic, many of which have been praised and promoted by Oprah herself. The two have bonded over some of the most discussed topics, and continue to do so to this very day.

30 Friends: Julia Roberts

29

Julia Roberts is probably one of the biggest figures in Hollywood that is able to say she is friends with the notable Oprah Winfrey! Julia has been a guest on Oprah's show more times than we can keep count of, which is a true testament to their friendship. The two are always spotted hugging and talking at major awards shows and events and the press love to get a shot of those very moments.

28 Friends: Beyoncé

27

Oprah Winfrey and Beyonce Knowles are without a doubt two names that just about anyone would know. Beyonce has been a guest Oprah's show since her Destiny's Child days, and the two have shared some pretty memorable moments with one another, including Beyoncé's iconic "Run The World" performance at Oprah's farewell show back in 2011.

26 Friends: Dr. Phil

25

Dr. Phil and Oprah first met back in 1995, when she had taken on Phil for a trial she was undergoing in the 90s. The two got rather close during her time, and it's noted that Oprah was so thrilled with Phil McGraw and the work he did that she wanted to get him a show of his own, which is where the entire Dr. Phil concept first began.

24 Friends: Steven Spielberg

23

Oprah and mega director Steven Spielberg first become friends when Oprah starred in Spielberg's film 'The Colour Purple'. The two have since been extremely close, and are often spotted catching up at events such as the Golden Globes and the Oscars. This is a power duo that we can definitely get behind.

22 Friends: Mariah Carey

21

When the queen of daytime talk show meets the queen of Christmas, we are in for quite the journey. Mariah Carey and Oprah have been friends since Mariah's debut in the early 90s. Carey has been a guest on 'The Oprah Show' many times and has graced the Oprah stage with a number of outstanding performances.
