Amber Rose Buys L.A. Strip Club Ace Of Diamonds

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Amber Rose went from stripping in a club to now owning a strip club. The How To Be A Bad Bitch author announced Wednesday night (Feb. 22) that she just bought the popular gentlemen’s club Ace of Diamonds because…well, why the hell not? Rose broke the news while accepting her award at Russell Simmon’s All Def Movie Awards ceremony in Los Angeles.

The savvy business move is yet another extension of Muva’s brand. On top of her annual SlutWalk, her nighttime talk show, tour, and management company adding a strip club to her business portfolio isn’t a bad investment at all.

Aside from her new business acquisition, Amber and Wiz Khalifa recently celebrated Sebastian’s fourth birthday. The former couple were all smiles as they dressed up as super hero’s for their son’s special day. Rumors began to swirl the two may have been getting back together, but Amber quickly stopped them insisting she and Wiz will always be family.

Either way, good looks on the business move, Rose.
