Who is the wife of Todd Haley? This article covers everything we know about one of the head coaches of the USFL.

Todd Haley is an American football coach.

He represents Tampa Bay Bandits of the United States Football League as its Head Coach and General Manager.

Similarly, Todd previously worked for New York Jets, Chicago Bears, and Dallas Cowboys as the wide receivers coach. Likewise, he was the offensive coordinator of the Arizona Cardinals (2007-2008), Pittsburgh Steelers (2012-2017), and Cleveland Brown (2018). Further, he was the head coach of Kansas City Cheifs (2009-2011).
