Kid Cudi Doesn't Think He'll Be Friends With Kanye West Again

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Kanye West has burned a lot of bridges in the last few years. In 2019, John Legend revealed that politics had put a strain on their relationship. Early this 2022, the Donda artist also had a social media meltdown wherein he attacked Pete Davidson who dated Kim Kardashian from November 2021 to August 2022.

The Saturday Night Live star was even buried alive in the rapper's music video for Eazy. Amid the online trolling, West also called out Kid Cudi for being friends with Davidson. Now, the Pursuit of Happiness rapper said that he can't be friends with the Yeezy founder again. Here's a timeline of their broken friendship.

How Did Kanye West & Kid Cudi Become Friends?

The two met a few years before Cudi signed to West's G.O.O.D Music Label in 2008. In 2013, Cudi eventually left the label but clarified that he and West didn't have a falling out.

"Man, everything is cool," he said back then. "Everything is peace with everybody on the label. There's never been any issues, but I am announcing that I'm no longer on G.O.O.D. Music." He explained that he and the Jeen-Yuhs star were simply headed in different directions.

But the next year, Cudi called out the billionaire for using his vocals on Guilt Trip, a track from his acclaimed album Yeezus. "The vocal that I did on that song was a couple years old. I forgot which session it was, but it was just a reference," he said at the time. He didn't know West had used his vocals until internet sleuths had a trending discussion about it on Twitter. The Day 'N' Nite hitmaker initially felt "flattered" but later realized that he should have been properly featured in the song.

"Then I started thinking about it more," he continued. "It was like, 'Why not call me and have me come in there and give it? Why underuse me? Why put four bars of vocals to coax my fans into thinking this is a legitimate Kid Cudi feature on this song and it isn't?'" The two engaged in a Twitter war for a while. But in September 2016, Cudi appeared on West's Saint Pablo Tour where they let fans know that they've made up.
