Why Did John Cena And Nikki Bella Break Up?

For those that watched Nikki Bella and John Cena threw the lens of sports entertainment, the break up was very hard to digest. Especially given all the ups and downs they all faced along the way.

Ultimately, Nikki Bella revealed that she felt as though she had lost herself during the relationship. In addition, the reality star felt as though the two did not want the same things for the future, like starting a family.

"Even in the end when he was willing to give me kids, I could just tell it's not what we wanted. And that's really, like, you know, what pushed me in the end, was like, if I'm going to force someone to be a father," she explained.

"I'll never forget, I think it was my life coach, he goes, 'What if he looks at you down the road and just regrets everything, and then you have this child and you've built this life? Is that what you want?' And I remember thinking, that's not what I want. I don't want to force someone to change."

The split was done on amicable terms and it was even filmed for Total Bellas. Shortly after, Nikki announced a relationship alongside her DWTS partner, and it had fans questioning her time on the competition.

Artem Chigvintsev Admitted To Crushing On Nikki Bella During 'Dancing With The Stars'

During DWTS, Nikki Bella and John Cena were still very much together. Nonetheless, Artem Chigvintsev admitted, he had instant feelings for Nikki from the first moment her met her.

“Favorite memory of us…well, obviously, I know—I mean, Nicole might feel a little different—but to me, just the [first] meeting, I think that was the one thing that kind of, like, tricked me out."

"I didn’t really understand what it meant for me at the time, but it’s just kind of like when you meet the certain person that you don’t really understand how you feel yet, you have this overwhelming—a lot of feelings and you don’t know how to cope with it and you don’t know what to do with it and you’re just trying to manage it as much as you can in the best way you can."

We can only imagine how awkward things must've been for Artem when John Cena showed up to their performance... even then, we can tell the chemistry between the two.

This leads to the next question, did Nikki feel the same way and did she cheat on her ex?

Nikki Bella Stated That Her Relationship With Artem Chigvintsev Was Professional During DWTS

Nikki Bella learned to love her coach from DWTS, however, she did reveal that things were strictly professional and in fact, she also went through a stage in which she strongly disliked her coach and future partner!

"He kind of made a comment about my jive and got really mad at me," she recalled. "And I was like 'You know what, dude? J. Lo and Beyoncé don't jive. I don't have time for this.' And I walked out and I got in my car."

Nikki recalls calling John Cena after the moment took place and threatening to quit the show altogether.

"I remember during dress rehearsals sometimes I'd be like, 'Is he mad at me?' " Bella recalled. "It would make me feel really off. It would just be his tone. It was hard, it was hard on me a lot," she further stated.

Nikki revealed that they are still working through communication skills, signing up for therapy. In addition, they are in the midst of planning their wedding, which has been delayed a few times.
