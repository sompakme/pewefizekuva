Hailey Bieber's 10 Most Expensive Outfits

Hailey Bieber, has known her way around the red carpet since she was a child. Coming from a family of actors, she was usually spotted attending movie premieres with her father, Stephen Baldwin. After being signed as an IMG Model, she has worked for fashion houses such as Ralph Lauren, Tommy Hilfiger, and Guess to star in their advertisements and walk the runway at fashion shows. Over the years, she has gone from wearing feminine silhouette pieces to daring yet elegant dresses with the help of her stylist Maeve Reiley. In 2021, Bieber joined hands with stylist Karla Welch to bring a new splash to her red carpet style.

From Galas to After-parties, Hailey Bieber has never disappointed anyone with her eye-popping red carpet looks that have become a topic of discussion everywhere. While the starlet has attended numerous events over the years, let’s take a look at some of the most expensive and noteworthy outfits worn by Hailey Bieber.

2016 Harper’s Bazaar Icons Party

During the 2016 New York Fashion Week, the Harper’s Bazaar Icons Party had a star-studded lineup with Kylie Jenner, Kendall Jenner, and Hailey Bieber gracing the red carpet. Dressed to the nines, Bieber donned a sheer shimmering long-sleeved Naeem Khan dress with open-back and stunning silver Stuart Weitzman heels.

2017 Met Gala

With the 2017 Met Gala theme as avant-garde, celebrities used their creative sides to bring the best outfits to the red carpet. Hailey Bieber looked like a futuristic ballerina with a full-layered tulle skirt and bustier custom-designed by artist Carolina Herrera, as stated by Vogue. With a winged eye-liner, net veil designed by Gigi Burris, she completed her outfit with Lorraine Schwartz jewelry.

2019 Met Gala

In a year that saw more outfit changes on the red carpet than ever before, the 2019 Met Gala stayed in line with the Camp Theme. Dressed in a custom Alexander Wang, Bieber wore a light-pink, long-sleeved sequined gown with a low back that exposed her thong. Similar to the gown's color, it had the word Wang embroidered on it in sequins. She had paired her outfit with Bulgari jewels.

2020 Vanity Fair Oscar After-Party

Hailey Bieber dazzled at the 2020 Vanity Fair Oscar After-Party in a black lingerie-like dress designed by Versace. The sheer dress came with long sleeves and a high thigh split that accentuated her look. She had paired the outfit with a pair of Jimmy Choo heels and Tiffany & Co. drop earrings, as noted by Just Jared.

2017 MTV Video And Music Awards

Dominating the red carpet at the 2017 MTV Video And Music Awards, Hailey Bieber shimmered in a silver plunging jumpsuit by Zuhair Murad. Her outfit was accessorized with a matching oversized belt while she had styled her necklace and rings with earrings by Jennifer Fisher. She was invited to the event to help introduce DNCE to the stage.

2018 amfAR Gala

Attending the 2018 amfAR Gala, Hailey Bieber looked chic and elegant in a Roberto Cavalli Fall/Winter 2017-2018 gown. The albino leopard print featured sequins and a patchwork bodice with flower embroidered elements. The gown was layered with sequins, lace, and feathers, and she finished the look with Lorraine Schwartz's jewelry.

2018 Vanity Fair Oscar After-Party

2018 was an eventful year for Hailey Bieber as the model traveled across Europe to walk the runways for major fashion houses during the fashion season. For the Oscars After-Party hosted by Vanity Fair, Bieber chose a custom-Versace number, a rose gold chainmail dress that made heads turn on the blue and white red carpet. The chainmail dress was paired with Forevermark diamonds.

2018 iHeartRadio Music Awards

Hailey Bieber dominated the 2018 iHeartRadio Music Awards because of her multiple outfit changes and as the host of the show alongside DJ Khaled. The ceremony saw a lot of celebrities following the trend of metallic dresses and sheer bodysuits, Bieber wasn’t far behind. As mentioned by PopSugar, she was dressed in a gold metallic jumpsuit by Zuhair Murad from the Spring 2018 Couture Collection. She completed the look with metallic Jimmy Choo heels, Lorraine Schwartz’s caviar ring, and 18-carat gold diamond earrings.

2018 Cannes Film Festival

Hailey Bieber brought glamour to the 2018 Cannes Film Festival as she made her way to the Girl In The Sun premiere red carpet. The model looked sensational in a nude embellished fishtail gown with a strapless neckline. Embellished with glittering sequins, the couture gown was designed by Roberto Cavalli. She had completed the look with an eye-catching diamond bracelet on her wrist.

2021 Met Gala

A regular at the Met Gala, 2021 was no different when the Biebers stepped out for their first Met Gala appearance since the pandemic. The couple cut a chic and elegant figure with outfits that compliment each other. According to Harper’s Bazaar, the model was dressed in a custom black Saint Laurent column gown with a plunging V-neckline studded with crystals. She had accessorized her look with a Tiffany & Co. square diamond necklace.

Hailey Bieber has turned heads with her red carpet appearances, from embellished and jeweled outfits to chic yet straightforward choices. The star knows how to carry herself elegantly, whether she is walking the runway, presenting an award, or being a supportive wife at movie and music premiere.
