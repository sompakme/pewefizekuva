  • 1987: Brooklyn Decker is born in Kettering, Ohio.
  • 2003: At 16, Decker begins her modelling career, signing with an agency in North Carolina.
  • 2006: Decker lands her first central magazine cover, appearing on the front of Sports Illustrated’s Swimsuit Issue.
  • 2007: Decker makes her acting debut, guest-starring on the television show “Chuck.”
  • 2010: Decker appears in her first significant film role, playing opposite Adam Sandler and Jennifer Aniston in “Just Go with It.”
  • 2012: Decker stars in the science fiction film “Battleship” alongside Liam Neeson and Rihanna.
