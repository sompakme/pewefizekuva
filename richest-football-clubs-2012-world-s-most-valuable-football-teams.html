Forbes.com released the 2012 list of world's most valuable football teams. Manchester United have retained their status as the world's richest club, with their value having soared by an estimated 20 per cent according FORBES. Manchester United are now worth $2.24 billion (€1.7bn) after posting record figures following their run to last season's Champions League Final.
