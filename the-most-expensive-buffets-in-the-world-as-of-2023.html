The Most Expensive Buffets In The World, As Of 2023

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Highlights

  • Buffets offer the opportunity to explore a wide variety of international cuisine in one place, making it an exciting gastronomic adventure.
  • Luxury buffet dining, although expensive, remains popular among food enthusiasts as it allows them to sample a variety of delicious dishes from different culinary heritages.
  • These world-class buffets are culinary jewels that provide guests with a luxurious experience, showcasing diverse fine foods prepared by talented chefs. The cost reflects the quality and atmosphere of these exceptional dining establishments.

Buffets have captured the imagination of food lovers all over the world, and provide them with the chance to sample a multitude of gourmet offerings. They offer an extraordinary chance to taste dishes from different parts of the world, by providing a broad range of international cuisine in one establishment.

It is the variety of tastes that one can choose from that make the meal an exciting gastronomic exploration. However, all buffets cannot be considered posh.

Luxury establishments that provide very expensive dining experiences are often found in elite hotels or exclusive restaurants. Although it is quite expensive, luxury buffet dining remains popular among gourmet enthusiasts as an opportunity to sample a variety of delicious dishes.

Here are some examples of the world's most lavish buffets, as of 2023.

11 Wicked Spoon Buffet At The Cosmopolitan Of Las Vegas

Located in The Cosmopolitan hotel in Las Vegas, this popular establishment offers an enticing weekend brunch priced reasonably at approximately $49 per person. Alongside delightful feasts from American, Asian, Italian, and Mexican cuisines, guests can enjoy unlimited bottomless beverage options for an additional $24.

10 The Wynn Buffet

The Wynn Buffet presents an extravagant opportunity that can enhance your visit to Las Vegas. When you opt to stay at The Wynn during your time in Sin City, you unlock exclusive benefits such as priority seating and the convenience of online buffet payment.

Even if you haven't booked a room with The Wynn, you have the chance to savor our exceptional dining experience. But you will have to prepare yourself financially to allocate roughly $50 to $70, for access to an extensive selection of over 90 delectable dishes spanning sixteen diverse cooking stations.

9 Eight Buffet At Cordis Auckland

Cordis Auckland's Eight Buffet stands out as one of New Zealand's finest offerings, costing around 129 New Zealand dollars.

It, too, is specifically tailored for Sunday brunch lovers — featuring sparkling wine throughout the experience, alongside refreshing juices and aromatic teas – complementing delectable international cuisine, spanning dim sum wonders, succulent roast lamb carvings, and a vast array of culinary delights.

8 The Line Buffet At Shangri-La Hotel Singapore

Shangri-La Hotel Singapore's The Line Buffet is an immensely popular choice among epicureans who appreciate quality indulgence. Priced at approximately 138 Singapore dollars, one can relish champagne or wine while exploring mesmerizing creations – served across various cuisines, diverse cooking stations, and in a contemporary and spacious setting boasting natural light through floor-to-ceiling windows.

7 Mezza9 Buffet At Grand Hyatt Singapore

The Mezza9 Buffet at Grand Hyatt Singapore is known as one of Asia's most luxurious buffet experiences, costing around 288 Singapore dollars for their Sunday champagne brunch event.

This lavish feast encompasses Japanese sushi and sashimi, Chinese dim sum, and Western favorites like roast duck and pasta delights – along with an array of irresistible desserts, served from nine bustling cooking stations.

6 Ritz-Carlton Singapore Sunday Brunch

Singapore's Ritz-Carlton invites food enthusiasts to experience their prestigious Sunday Brunch, priced approximately at 388 Singapore dollars — an exquisite display of culinary indulgence.

Dishes include exceptional seafood options including as lobster and caviar, salmon sensations, and succulent Wagyu beef complemented by delectable duck masterpieces — all within an elegant dining hall adorned with chandeliers, providing a touch of old-world class.

5 Saffron Brunch Buffet At Atlantis, The Palm

Dubai's Atlantis, The Palm offers its luxurious Saffron Brunch Buffet, an immersive gastronomic journey valued at approximately 535 dirhams per person, on Fridays.

Offering an extensive selection of items sourced globally, this buffet boasts more than twenty live cooking stations – showcasing Asian delicacies, combined harmoniously against European influences, heightened further through Middle East inspirations.

This culminates in stunning coastal wonders, tandoori chicken alongside tempting sushi creations, and seductive sashimi selections. It concludes beautifully with delectable dessert options, a culinary delight set amidst beautifully arranged surroundings and guided by skilled DJs enhancing the entire experience.

4 Conrad’s Hong Kong Sunday Brunch Buffet

Conrad Hong Kong presents its grand Sunday Brunch Buffet as one of the most expensive experiences in the region — priced approximately at 1,088 Hong Kong dollars per person, and inclusive of free-flowing champagne, wine, beer, and cocktails.

This costly spread features dishes from different international cuisines; Chinese or Japanese selections featuring delightful dim sum wonders and sushi/sashimi delicacies, harmonized with French and Italian flavors.

Curry specialties, cheese boards showcasing exquisite options like foie gras terrine, and sumptuous roast beef are among offerings, all within an elegant space providing panoramic views overlooking Victoria Harbour.

3 Zemi Beach House 20 Knots Breakfast Buffet

To enjoy the most expensive and luxurious buffets in the world, you may need to do some traveling. For instance, there exists an exorbitantly expensive breakfast buffet nestled within Anguilla, a petite Caribbean haven known for its serene ambiance, sun-kissed beaches, and gastronomic delights.

This particular opulent feast is conveniently housed within a luxurious hotel setting, like numerous other distinguished buffets scattered across the globe. Consequently, partaking in this indulgent morning spread necessitates being an esteemed patron of this establishment by virtue of reserving accommodations.

Fortunately for guests, this awe-inspiring breakfast experience comes as a gratuitous offering along with their stay; as such, no additional expenditure is required to bask in the lap of luxury at this magnificent buffet.

Nonetheless, it's crucial to note that securing a room at Zemi Beach House can range anywhere between $650 and $4,500 per night. However, this exquisite buffet outing may very well prove to be the pinnacle of extravagance for lucky visitors.

2 Harborside Buffet At InterContinental Hong Kong

InterContinental Hong Kong presents the extravagant Harborside Buffet is an unparalleled journey priced at around 1,188 Hong Kong dollars per person during Sunday brunch service. Guests can indulge in unlimited champagne, complemented by a selection of beers and wines, while being surrounded by breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour.

The buffet features lavish seafood options including lobster, caviar, crab, salmon, and tuna, along with prime cuts like Wagyu beef. Guests can end the experience on a high note, with perfectly prepared duck dishes — a truly luxurious setting complete with unforgettable panoramic views.

1 Le Blanc Spa Resort's Los Cabos Breakfast Buffet

Begin your day luxuriously at Le Blanc Spa Resort Los Cabos' Breakfast Buffet in Mexico, for approximately $1,400 per person (inclusive of one night's stay).

Indulge in a wide array of delectable options such as fresh fruits, pastries, eggs cooked to perfection (including omelets), pancakes, or waffles adorned generously bacon or sausages; limitless champagne glasses alongside coffee, tea, and juice, flawlessly served amid elegant surroundings (and offering picturesque ocean views). These buffets are culinary jewels that present a collection of diverse fine foods, prepared by world-class cooks. The cost is proportionate to the journey across various gastronomic heritages, and reflective of an atmosphere carefully curated to provide guests with a truly luxurious experience.
