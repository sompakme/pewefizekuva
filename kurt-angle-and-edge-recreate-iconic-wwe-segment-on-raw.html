Kurt Angle And Edge Recreate Iconic WWE Segment On Raw

Publish date: 2024-09-18

Tonight, Kurt Angle made his return to the WWE on Monday Night Raw in his hometown of Pittsburgh. He faced off in the ring against Gable and Otis of the Alpha Academy and then sat at ringside for a match between the Academy and the Street Profits.

RELATED: The Rock Pays Tribute To Brock Lesnar On 20-Year Anniversary Of SummerSlam MatchBut the fun didn't stop for Angle after that. Later in the night, he appeared in a backstage segment alongside his longtime rival and fellow Hall of Famer, Edge.

Angle Gets Pranked, Again

About 20 years ago, Edge pranked Kurt Angle during an especially memorable episode of Smackdown. He met Angle backstage and showed him some pictures he had printed out of the two of them. Little did Kurt know that each photo had a message written on the back that the audience read each time Kurt held one up. The signs read "Yes I do suck," "And I'm a dork," and lastly, "I have no testicles."

On tonight's episode of Raw, Edge gave Angle a similar set of photographs. This time, the backs of the pictures had messages like "Sorry I shaved your head bald," and "Sorry I started the 'you suck' chant." The last one had an arrow pointing to Kurt's face and read "You still suck."

It wasn't until Angle went to show Rey Mysterio and Dominik the pictures that he saw the messages written on the back of the poster board. Angle, exasperated, shouted "He did it again!" and walked away. Nothing protects you from a prank, not even an Olympic gold medal.
