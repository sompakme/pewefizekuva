Poppy Montgomery, Shawn Sanford Secret Wedding: Disneyland, Jeans!

Publish date: 2024-07-26

Well, why not get married at the happiest place on Earth? Actress Poppy Montgomery secretly wed boyfriend Shawn Sanford at Disneyland months ago, she revealed on Live! With Kelly and Michael on Friday, April 4 — and none other than Mickey Mouse and Minnie Mouse presided!

"On Chinese New Year, I ran away with all of the children and got married at Disneyland," the Unforgettable actress, 38, began. (Montgomery and Sanford are parents to daughter Violet, 12 months; she's also mom to Jackson, 6, with ex-boyfriend Adam Kaufman, and Sanford has a young daughter from a previous relationship.)
