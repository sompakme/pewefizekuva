Christina Milian, Fiance Jas Prince Split: Singer Ends Engagement

Publish date: 2024-07-09

It's over. Christina Milian has called it quits with fiance Jas Prince after two years of dating, a rep for the star's family confirms exclusively to Us Weekly.

The singer/songwriter/actress, 32, called off her engagement to the Rap-A-Lot Records CEO earlier this month. She moved her stuff out of their shared house in L.A. on Thursday, June 19.

Milian and Prince reportedly started dating in 2010, months after her split from ex-husband The-Dream, with whom she has a 4-year-old daughter, Violet. In April 2013, she was spotted wearing a huge diamond ring—but she played coy about whether she was engaged when asked in interviews.

Tellingly, Milian took to Instagram on June 12 with a message about starting fresh and living the life you want. She shared a photo of a quote reading: "There comes a time in life, when you walk away from all the drama and people who create it. Surround yourself with people who make you laugh, forget the bad, and focus on the good. Love the people who treat you right. Pray for the ones who don't. Life is too short to be anything but happy. Falling down is part of life, getting back up is living."
