Insiders Think Josh And Anna Duggar Might Be Separating After All (And Keeping It Quiet)

Publish date: 2024-09-08

Highlights

  • Insider sources claim Anna Duggar is quietly preparing to divorce Josh and possibly seeking legal advice.
  • Anna's Instagram went dark, leading to speculation that she is trying to move quietly.
  • The Duggar family seems to believe Anna will never leave Josh and may still be offering financial support.

When fans of the Duggar family first found out about Josh Duggar's wrongdoings, many immediately began campaigning for his wife, Anna (née Keller) to divorce him. Pregnant with six children (their seventh was born while Josh was behind bars), Anna was pitied for her husband's crimes and what spectators considered a sad life with the Duggars.

Despite the public's criticism of Josh, Anna has publicly supported her husband, showing up to court, sharing positive commentary about Josh on social media, and continuing to affiliate with her in-laws. However, after she deleted her Instagram account and otherwise went dark on social media, fans were curious about what she was up to. Here, we'll cover the rumors about Anna Duggar possibly filing for divorce from Josh, what insiders say about Anna's ties to the Duggars, and where Anna is now.

One YouTube Channel Claims To Have Insider Info About Anna Duggar Filing For Divorce

Multiple "sources" claim that Anna is quietly making moves to separate from Josh Duggar

In an "Exclusive Anna & Josh Duggar Seperation Update!," a YouTube channel laid out tidbits of information about Josh and Anna Duggar, including the fact that the couple might be separating. However, the channel does not reveal its sources, nor does it give details on any of the apparent rumors about Josh and Anna.

One "fact" was that Anna Duggar was in contact with a family law attorney, which many speculate to mean that she was planning to divorce Josh.

Some hints at Anna's family possibly creating a trust fund for her is another complicating factor; Anna managing her assets with an attorney could be misconstrued as her needing legal counsel for a divorce.

Anna's net worth was $60,000 in 2023 .

The other piece of speculation from the same YouTube video was that an inmate close to Josh leaked that Josh suspected Anna wanted to leave him. While that doesn't confirm any legal action by Anna, if true, it might suggest that Josh Duggar is worried about his wife seeking a divorce.

If the leaked "facts" are indeed true, fans can expect that eventually, Anna Duggar will make a legal move that would create a paper trail proving her desire to divorce. However, the "leak" happened in late 2023, at which point Anna had already been seeing an attorney for three months, according to the 'inside source.'

In early 2024, Ann was still quietly married to Josh, and no legal paperwork has been flagged in the Arkansas court system (yet?).

After Josh Duggar's criminal trial and conviction, family members empathized with Anna

However, it's unclear whether Anna remains on good terms with the Duggars.

Anna Might Have Moved To Texas To Be Closer To Josh's Prison

Rumors of a fight with Jim Bob have Anna moving to Texas
