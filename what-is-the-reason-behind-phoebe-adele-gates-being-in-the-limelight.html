What Is The Reason Behind Phoebe Adele Gates Being In The Limelight?

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Phoebe Adele Gates is famous for being the youngest of three children of Bill Gates, who happens to be the second richest man in the world. 

He has a net worth of around 90 billion dollars. 

Biography 

Phoebe Adele Gates was born in Media, Washington State, on September 14, 2002.

Her zodiac sign is Virgo, and she is a citizen of the United States. She has Irish and German ancestors.

Her parents met while both working at Microsoft and raised Phoebe in Media.

Her mother, Melinda , reared her for the most part while her father was at work. Her father, Bill Gates, is a well-known businessman, philanthropist, and the founder of Microsoft, and he is the world’s second-richest person

On the other hand, her mother is a co-chairman of the “Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation.”

She went to New York’s Professional Children’s School and has been interested in dance and music since she was a child.

Phoebe has studied at the American Ballet Theatre, Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, and The Juilliard School.

She is presently enrolled at Lakeside School, which her father and brothers previously attended.

She studied art at American Ballet, New York’s Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, and Juilliard School. 

Phoebe is scheduled to graduate in the year 2021. She was always interested in technology, singing, and dancing. 

She frequently participates in her parents’ many humanitarian events. She avoids the press and never spends time on Instagram posting fancy photos.

She doesn’t use Instagram or any other social media platform. Her father was adamant about his kids using technology so that they could focus on their careers. 

Jennifer Katharine Gates is Phoebe’s sister, and Rory John Gates is her brother.

Despite the fact that Jennifer has moved away and just married, the sisters maintain a close bond. 

Jennifer uses social media more than Phoebe, who has always maintained a modest profile.

In an emotional Instagram message, she wished her younger sister a happy 18th birthday on September 14, 2020.

Phoebe adores both dancing and music, with rock and blues as her favorite genre and ballet as her favorite dance form. 

She also appreciates operas, which she regards as a beautiful blend of storyline and singing. Her interest in the film industry derives from her brother’s career as an actor.

Her favorite actors and actresses are Johnny Depp and Marilyn Monroe, and her favorite movies are “Edward Scissorhands,” “Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street,” and the “Pirates of the Caribbean” franchise.

Phoebe is also very sporty, as she competes in soccer and volleyball at school.

She adores photography and attends exhibitions in her hometown on a regular basis. She’s also equipped with a camera.

Phoebe is a seventeen-year-old woman. She stands 5ft 5ins (1.65m) tall, weighs 125 pounds, and has long brown hair and brown eyes (57kgs).

She is a beautiful young woman with a lovely smile and fair skin.

Phoebe was voted one of Wonders’ “World’s Top Ten Most Strong Girls.”

Phoebe’s Love For Reading Inherited By Her Father

Despite the fact that her mother mainly raised Phoebe, Bill significantly impacted her life. 

A shared love of reading is one of the many things they have in common. 

“My daughter Phoebe and I love reading all kinds of books and are big fans of @johngreenwritesbooks,” Bill wrote in an Instagram post in 2018, alongside a nice photo of the two. 

We couldn’t put his new novel, Turtles All the Way Down, down, and it sits about a young woman who goes on the hunt for a lost billionaire.”

“For years, I have been a faithful John Green fan – devouring his novels in the backs of coffee shops, while traveling, and snuggled up on my couch,” he said in the post. 

I get caught up in the fantasy of his novels because of the imagery in his works, but Turtles All the Way Down came more to reality for me than the others.”

Does Phoebe Still Live With Her Parents?

While her siblings have already moved out, Phoebe continues to live with her parents in their $120 million home, which includes a gym, a swimming pool with an underwater sound system, and an extensive library. 

The cost of maintaining the mansion for a year is estimated to be above $1 million, with yearly taxes costing even more.

What Is The Career Path For Phoebe Adele Gates?

Phoebe may be too young to begin a career in any sector, but she has stated that she intends to attend college after matriculation. 

She wants to be a professional dancer and singer, and she has $10 million to help her get started. 

Even though Bill Gates’ net wealth is enormous, he has permitted his children to receive $10 million each; his children are okay with this and have stated in an interview that they would like to make their own money anyhow. 

Jennifer, Phoebe’s younger sister, studied biology at Stanford University before deciding to pursue a career as an equestrian (horse rider). Rory, Phoebe’s brother, is an actor.

How was Phoebe Affected By Her Parent’s Divorce?

Phoebe was crucial in her parents’ divorce – or rather, its postponement. 

Melinda had long desired a divorce, but the couple didn’t divorce until all of their children were adults.

“The decision to split up appears to be tied to their youngest daughter, Phoebe, becoming 18 years old,” People reports. 

It’s because their youngest child is graduating from high school, and the plan was for them to be together during that time.”
