Former TNA Knockout / Shimmer Champion Joins Lucha Underground Roster, Lucha Undergrounds Melissa

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Lucha Underground Roster

The Wrestling Observer Newsletter is reporting Melissa Anderson will be joining the Lucha Underground roster for the second season, and will portray the sister of Marty the Moth (Martin Casaus).

Anderson, who currently wrestles as Cheerleader Melissa, is most known for her time with TNA Wrestling as Alissa Flash and Raisha Saeed, in addition to being a two-time Shimmer Women’s Champion. 

As noted, Lucha Underground’s second season will begin taping next month.
