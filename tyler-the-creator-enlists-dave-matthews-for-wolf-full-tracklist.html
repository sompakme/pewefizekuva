Tyler, the Creator Enlists Dave Matthews for Wolf (Full Tracklist)

Publish date: 2024-07-16

When he’s not loitering your television or causing street riots, Tyler, the Creator is just another rapper prepping the release of another album. The Golf Wang chief is set to drop his third solo LP Wolf on April 2. The follow-up to his breakthrough and Kanye West-hyped Goblin, Wolf will feature the usual Odd Future bunch (Frank Ocean, Left Brain, Domo Genesis, Hodgy Beats, Earl Sweatshirt) and one very unexpected guest — guitar crooning, frat-boy favorite Dave Matthews.

To say Tyler, the Creator has out done himself on this tracklist is an understatement. When the tracklist leaked last week, some questioned its legitimacy, but the LP’s Amazon page has since confirmed the details. Full 18-song tracklist follows Tyler’s “Domo23” video below.

Tyler, the Creator Wolf tracklist:
1. “Wolf”
2. “Jamba” (Feat. Hodgy Beats)
3. “Cowboy”
4. “Awkward”
5. “Domo23”
6. “Answer”
7. “Slater/Escape-ism” (Feat. Frank Ocean & Dave Matthews)
8. “48”
9. “Colossus/The Bridge of Love”
10. “Partyisntover/Campfire/Bimmer” (Feat. Lætitia Sadier & Frank Ocean)
11. “IFHY” (Feat. Pharrell)
12. “Pigs”
13. “Parking Lot” (Feat. Mike G & Casey Veggies)
14. “Rusty” (Feat. Domo Genesis & Earl Sweatshirt)
15. “Trashwang” (Feat. Na-kel, Jasper, Lucas, L-Boy, Taco, Left Brain & Lee Spielman)
16. “Treehome95” (Feat. Coco O. & Erykah Badu)
17. “Tamale” (Feat. Tallulah)
18. “Lone/Jornada”
