Doja Cat Mentions Iggy Azalea And Nicki Minaj From &quot;Hacked&quot; X Account

Publish date: 2024-09-28


Doja Cat has claimed that her X account was hacked on Monday (July 8) after a few tweets about Iggy Azalea and Nicki Minaj shocked her followers. Immediately, the “Agora Hills” artist shut down any beliefs that it was her writing on her account.

“My twitter’s been hacked, these messages are not from me,” she announced to her following.

Her message came after her account mentioned the Australian rapper. “F**k that stupid bi**h @IGGYAZALEA and buy $DOJA,” the tweet read.

In another post, her account mentioned Nicki Minaj, asking, “where you at boo?” and responded to controversial social media sensation Andrew Tate, writing, “hey boo.”

As Minaj nor Tate has responded to the “hacked” posts, Iggy wrote, “Get rugged if yall want but I’m cool w that girl irl so yall f**king up w that tweet hackers.”

She added in another post, “They hacked Doja cat n have posted a sh*tcoin scam. Hackers on celeb girl accounts again making it about me cause I’m their fixation but they didn’t consider the fact that I may actually interact w ppl in real life and it’s not fitting into reality lol.”

Doja Cat deactivated her Instagram back in March after claiming that she was being bullied online.

At the time, she wrote, “hey i’m gonna deactivate because im not really feeling this anymore. you guys take care of yourselves.” She then edited it to, “I like coming here to find inspo and see people being creative but i just feel this is getting to be too much.”

The 28-year-old added, “The way im spoken to on here and treated makes me have f**ked up thoughts. Please watch how you talk to and about people on the internet. Bye.”
