Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart were a hotly debated couple in Hollywood, captivating fans with their chemistry both on and off-screen. The pair first met on the set of Riverdale, the hit CW series that catapulted them into the spotlight. As Jughead and Betty, they portrayed a couple whose romance was as endearing as it was tumultuous.

Rumors of a real-life romance between the two began to swirl around the internet, and after some time, they confirmed the news themselves in 2018. Fans were ecstatic, and it didn't take long for Cole and Lili to become one of the most adored couples in the young Hollywood scene.

However, their love story came to an unexpected end in March 2020, when they announced their split. Fans were left shocked by the news, but it wasn't until recently that the internet began to turn on Cole Sprouse.
