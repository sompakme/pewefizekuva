Diane Kruger has a net worth of $24 million. She is a renowned model and a German actress.

She has made her wealth from her roles in several movies. Diane also has endorsements from several companies like L’Oreal. She recently was noticed in her role as Dr Abigail Chase in National Treasure. Her performance in the roles of Bridget Von in Inglorious Basterds and Gina in Unknown became widely appreciated by the audience and also by the critics.

She started her modeling career in the year 1992. She represented the agency, Elite Model Look of Germany. Kruger did modeling for a relatively short time, but at that time, she managed to do advertisements for prestigious companies such as Chanel, Salvatore Ferragamo, Giorgio Armani, and Louis Vuitton. These commercials made people take notice of her. She appeared on the covers of magazines like Vogue, Cosmopolitan and others like this. Diane, however, stopped her modeling career once she decided that she would pursue a career in acting. She took acting lessons at the Cours Florent and went on to play small roles in several French films. She made her debut in “The Piano Player,” which was a television movie. She received notice when she played the role of Helen of Sparta in “Troy”.

She was born on 15th July 1976 in Algermissen, Germany. In 1999, she began dating the French actor and director Guillaume Canet. They got married on 1st September 2001, but the couple got divorced after just five years. The reason behind the divorce was said to be their respective careers. Both of them were so busy that they hardly had time to see each other. She has been in a relationship with Norman Reedus since 2016.
