&quot;Ain't No Mystery In My History&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Lil Baby addressed speculation that he was involved in a leaked gay sex tape. On Sunday (Oct. 22), the Atlanta rapper took to his Instagram Story to release a statement regarding the footage. He asserted he was “sick” of people using his name to chase “clout” and attention. Baby proclaimed that there wasn’t any “cap in his rap” and denied being the man in the video.

“Y’all gotta stop using my name and likeness when y’all get bored, then the extremes mfs go to for clout is sick [angry face emojis]!! Ain’t no mystery in my history on NO LEVEL,” he typed. “This my last time addressing any kind of dumb a** click bait. Tf is wrong wit y’all [facepalm emoji]. No flaws, no cap in my raps.”

The video in question showed a mad with braids similar to Baby’s giving another man oral sex. Fans began to weigh in on the video, alleging that the man was the “In a Minute” rapper. Social media users flocked to his Instagram to crack jokes and fling obscenities at the entertainer over the viral video.

“Damn, gang you suck better than my homegirls,” someone commented on IG. “Look at lil lady. Ni**as get in the industry and turn sweet,” another social media user declared. “Needa change his name to lil disappointment, bc now I gotta edit my playlist and take you out,” another person shot at the rapper.

However, one of Lil Baby’s adversaries did surprisingly come to his defense. DJ Akademiks responded to the viral video on Twitter on Sunday night. “MAN WAT DA F**K… I can’t even google a ni**a for some raps no more.. wat type of sh*t yall got on here. I was tryna Look up da Freestyle… ‘Shout out my label dats me’… I Found sum whole otha sht… WTF,” he xweeted. “Lil Baby innocent… yall stop playing w/ him. 4PF aint w/ the LGBTQ shenanigans. They goons.”
