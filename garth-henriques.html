Garth Henriques is the father of Well-established Jamaican dancehall rapper, singer, and record producer, Sean Paul.

Married life with the wife was good

Garth Henriques was married to Frances Henriques. However, there is less information available about them. 

The details about the wedding day and their dating life are hidden from the media. 

His wife was a painter by profession while he was a swimmer and even played water polo for the team in the 1960s. 

It would not be wrong to say that everybody in his family is an expert at swimming. His dad was the first Jamaican men’s national water polo team, and even his wife was a butterfly swimmer. 

The couple shared the same kind of values and cultures as they were both the natives of Jamaica. After marriage, the couple shared two children. 

Both of them are boys whose names are Sean Paul and Jason Jigzagula Henriques. His conjugal life with his wife and children was good.

Died on 24th of August,2018

Garth Henriques was a dotting father, a good husband to his wife, and caring &loving to his parents. He took his last breath on the 24th of August,2018. 

Since April, he was not keeping well with his health and was admitted to The University Hospital of the West Indies.

Many people even donated blood but could not save his life. However, detailed information about his health was not shared with the public. 

The grown-up in the multi-ethnic family

His sons Sean Paul and Jason Jigzagula Henriques grown up in a multi-ethnic family as their paternal grandfather had Portuguese heritage and grandmother had Sephardic Jewish Heritage.

And not only that, his maternal grandfather was African, and Grandmother had Chinese heritage. So basically, they are half Portuguese, African and Chinese.  

But for them, it must be enjoyable to be grown up knowing such different cultures and values.
