Who Is George Lopez Daughter, Mayan Lopez?

Publish date: 2024-09-26

George Lopez has certainly had a career worth celebrating! The comedian turn actor has done just about everything. From the stars time doing stand-up, all the way to his hit sitcom, George Lopez.

The series, which ran for a whopping six seasons, came to an end just in time for Lopez to get his very own talk show, Lopez Tonight. In addition to being a big name in the business, Lopez is also just as great a father.

The star welcomed his daughter, Mayan Lopez back in 1996 and the 25-year-old appears to be following in her father's footsteps. So, who is George Lopez' daughter, Mayan? Let's find out!

Who Is Mayan Lopez?

Mayan Lopez is certainly no stranger to the public eye! The 25-year-old has been used to the limelight ever since birth considering her father is George Lopez and all.

Mayan pops up quite a lot on her father's social media, particularly this past month following their Father's Day celebrations. George Lopez posted a photo of himself and baby Mayan Lopez swimming together nearly 24 years ago, sparking fans to wonder just who his true pride and joy really are.

Lopez has experienced the entertainment business firsthand, visiting dad on-set of his hit sitcom, George Lopez, all while appearing on the series a handful of times in the role of Jackie.

Fans have also seen Mayan grow up on the red carpet, attending events and premieres by her side, including their very last while Mayan was still young back in 2013 during the Smurfs premiere.

Today, Mayan is quite popular on social media and has garnered quite the following on TikTok! Lopez is known for her witty and viral comedic posts, which oftentimes touch on her celebrity life and upbringing.

One series Mayan has online is rating past photos of herself meeting other celebrities through her dad. From Sandra Bullock, Justin Bieber, all the way to Samuel L. Jackson, Mayan has a fun way of creating content, leaving us to wonder if she'll follow in her dad's footsteps.

Will She Follow In Dad's Footsteps?

Although fans are curious if Mayan will also dabble in acting like her father, it appears as if she already has!

The star has appeared in a handful of films and TV shows outside of her appearances on George Lopez. In 2009, Mayan landed a small role in the TV film, Mr. Troop Mom.

Nearly a decade later, Lopez appeared in Handsome: A Netflix Mystery Movie, all while snagging two additional roles a year later in TMI Hollywood and Jersey.

Now, Mayan is taking her career to the next level by not only following her dad's footsteps but by quite literally appearing alongside him, again!

Well, 14 years following their first on-screen moment together on George's sitcom, the duo are set to appearing together in Lopez vs. Lopez, a working-class family multi-camera comedy, which is set to be air on NBC.

The show came about via The Conners executive producer, Bruce Helford, who also co-created George Lopez, alongside comedian and co-executive producer, Debby Wolfe.
